Ridley Graduate Boat Club

Est. 1972

Built on tradition, forged for the future.

The Ridley Graduate Boat Club (RGBC) is committed to fostering excellence and high performance across all age groups and skill levels. Whether you're a junior athlete discovering the sport, a university rower training over the summer, a masters athlete, or an athlete training to represent their country, RGBC offers a welcoming and challenging environment tailored to your goals. Our programming is designed to support both recreational rowers and those striving for provincial, national, or international success, creating space for personal growth, athletic development, and lasting connection to the sport.


Operating from the renowned Henley Island in St. Catharines, Ontario—home to one of the most iconic rowing venues in North America—RGBC provides its members with access to top-tier equipment, a comprehensive training environment, and experienced, professional coaching. The club fosters a team culture built on mutual respect, accountability, and the pursuit of excellence. Whether training for a local regatta or the national team, our athletes benefit from a supportive community that values hard work, resilience, and a shared passion for rowing and our club.
