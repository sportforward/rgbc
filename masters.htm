Masters Program

❌ Not Currently Accepting New Athletes

The Masters program at RGBC is for adults who want to stay active, improve their rowing skills, and enjoy the camaraderie of a supportive team. Whether you are a seasoned competitor or new to the sport, our program offers a welcoming environment for all experience levels. Training sessions are tailored to individual goals, from fitness and recreation to racing at local and national regattas.

Masters rowers benefit from expert coaching, flexible training options, and a vibrant club community. The program emphasizes fun, fitness, and friendship, making it a great way to stay healthy, challenge yourself, and build lasting connections on and off the water.
