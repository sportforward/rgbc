U23/Senior Program

✅ Now Accepting Athletes

The U23/Senior program at RGBC is for athletes who are committed to high-performance rowing and are looking to take their training and racing to the next level. Our program offers a year-round training environment, individualized coaching, and access to top-tier equipment. Athletes are encouraged to set ambitious goals, whether that's making a university team, competing at the national level, or representing Canada internationally.


In addition to rigorous training, the program emphasizes leadership, resilience, and the importance of team culture. Athletes support each other, push their limits, and grow both as rowers and as individuals. The U23/Senior group is a place for driven athletes to challenge themselves and achieve their full potential in a supportive, competitive environment.
