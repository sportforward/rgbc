Nancy Storrs
Ontario Indoor Rowing Championships

Welcome to the Nancy Storrs Ontario Indoor Rowing Championships, one of the oldest and most prestigious indoor rowing events in Canada. Originally founded as the Ontario Ergometer Championships in the 1980s by the legendary Nancy Storrs, this championship has been renamed to honor her vision, dedication, and leadership in the sport. Nancy was a two-time Olympian, World Championship medalist, and longtime organizer of this event, making it a fitting tribute to her remarkable contributions to rowing in Canada.


The 2026 edition will be held at the Ridley College Fieldhouse in St. Catharines, Ontario, featuring both traditional 2000m races and exciting 500m sprints for U13 and U15 athletes. This inclusive event welcomes rowers from schools, clubs, or as unaffiliated participants, ensuring that everyone can experience the thrill of indoor rowing competition. With 495 entries from 32 clubs expected, the championship continues to grow while maintaining its commitment to fostering the next generation of rowers. New in 2025, spectators could watch the action live through the Ridley College HUDL Stream, bringing the excitement of indoor rowing to audiences beyond the venue.
