U19/Youth Program

✅ Now Accepting New Athletes

The U19/Youth program at RGBC is tailored for young athletes who are eager to learn, grow, and compete in the sport of rowing. Our program welcomes both beginners and experienced rowers, providing a structured environment where athletes can develop their technique, fitness, and confidence. Training sessions are designed to be challenging yet supportive, helping each athlete progress at their own pace.


In addition to on-water training, the program includes land-based workouts, team-building activities, and opportunities to race at local and regional regattas. Our coaches foster a positive atmosphere where youth athletes are encouraged to set goals, support their teammates, and enjoy the journey of becoming well-rounded rowers and individuals.
