Highschool Program

Our highschool rowing program is designed for student-athletes who want to develop their skills, fitness, and teamwork in a supportive and challenging environment. Whether you are new to rowing or have previous experience, our coaches will help you build a strong foundation and reach your potential. Training sessions focus on technical development, strength, and endurance, preparing athletes for competition at the local, regional, and national levels.


We work with the local highschools to provide a program that is tailored to the needs of the students. If your school is interested in having a rowing program without having to manage the logistics, please contact us at ridleygraduateboatclub@gmail.com and we will get back to you as soon as possible.


Beyond the water, the program emphasizes leadership, sportsmanship, and personal growth. Athletes form lasting friendships, learn to work as a team, and develop the confidence to take on new challenges. Our highschool rowers are encouraged to set goals, support one another, and represent RGBC with pride both on and off the water.
