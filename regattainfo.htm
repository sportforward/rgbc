Regatta Information
  • Registration Deadlines
    • Entries typically due 2-3 weeks before event
    • Late entries may incur additional fees
    • Scratches will be paid by the members in the crew scratched
    • Check individual regatta websites for specific deadlines (hover over regatt names)

  • Transportation
    • Club will coordinate equipment transportation for most events
    • Equipment loading times posted 1 week prior
    • Departure times vary by event location

  • Equipment
    • Club boats available for regatta use
    • Personal equipment must be approved by coaches
    • Safety equipment provided by club
